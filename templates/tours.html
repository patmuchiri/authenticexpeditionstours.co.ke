Samburu National Reserve

Discover the rugged beauty of Northern Kenya

From USD 650 per person (min 4 pax)

Experience

Located in Northern Kenya, Samburu is renowned for its rugged beauty, arid landscapes, and unique wildlife. This reserve is home to rare species like Grevy's zebra, Somali ostrich, and reticulated giraffe, making it perfect for those seeking adventure off the beaten path.

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Early departure | Scenic stop at Mt. Ololokwe | Arrival in Samburu | Cultural experience with Samburu warriors | Dinner & overnight
  • Day 2: Early morning game drive | Afternoon visit to Reteti Elephant Sanctuary | Return to camp for dinner
  • Day 3: Breakfast | Optional nature walk | Begin return journey with lunch stop en route
