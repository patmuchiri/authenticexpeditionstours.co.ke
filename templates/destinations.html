Naivasha Day Trip

Boat rides and wildlife on beautiful Lake Naivasha

From USD 90 per person (min 6 pax)

Experience

Explore Lake Naivasha's serene beauty and its rich birdlife on this perfect day trip for nature lovers and adventure seekers. The highlight is Crescent Island, a predator-free sanctuary where you can walk among zebras, giraffes, and antelopes with stunning lake views.

Unique Feature: Crescent Island is one of the few places in Africa where you can walk freely among wildlife without vehicle restrictions, offering an intimate safari experience.

Activities

  • Scenic drive through the Great Rift Valley
  • Boat ride on Lake Naivasha to see hippos and birds
  • Guided walking safari on Crescent Island
  • Optional visit to Hell's Gate National Park
  • Lunch at a lakeside restaurant

Includes

  • Round-trip transport from Nairobi
  • Boat ride on Lake Naivasha
  • Entry fees to Crescent Island
  • Professional guide services
  • Bottled water and lunch

Excludes

  • Personal expenses and souvenirs
  • Additional activities not mentioned
  • Tips for guides and boat crew

Aberdare National Park

Misty forests and waterfalls in the highlands

From USD 45 per person (min 6 pax)

Experience

Discover the breathtaking Aberdare ranges with their mist-covered forests, spectacular waterfalls, and diverse wildlife. This high-altitude park offers a completely different experience from Kenya's savannah parks, with lush vegetation and cool mountain air.

Eco-System: The Aberdare ranges are part of Kenya's water catchment system, feeding several major rivers and providing Nairobi with most of its water supply.

Activities

  • Guided game drives through diverse habitats
  • Visit to the stunning Chania and Karuru waterfalls
  • Bird watching (over 250 species recorded)
  • Short nature walks in designated areas
  • Picnic lunch with mountain views

Includes

  • Round-trip transport from Nyeri
  • Game drives as per itinerary
  • Professional guide services
  • Bottled water

Excludes

  • Park entry fees
  • Specialized hikes (available on request)
  • picnic lunch on request
  • Personal expenses
  • Tips for guides and drivers
