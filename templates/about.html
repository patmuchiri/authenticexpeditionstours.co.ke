Our Mission

At Authentic Expeditions, we're dedicated to providing personalized and enriching travel experiences that showcase Kenya's spectacular cultural and natural heritage while delivering exceptional customer satisfaction at every turn.

We believe travel should be transformative, creating connections between people, cultures, and the natural world that last a lifetime.

Our Vision

We aspire to be recognized as Kenya's premier travel company, renowned for crafting authentic, immersive, and sustainable travel experiences that set the standard for responsible tourism in East Africa.

Our goal is to expand our impact across the region while maintaining the personal touch that defines the Authentic Expeditions experience.

Our Core Values

These fundamental principles guide every decision we make and every experience we create:

Customer-Centric

Sustainability

We're committed to eco-friendly practices that protect Kenya's natural beauty for future generations.

Cultural Appreciation

We celebrate and promote Kenya's rich traditions through respectful, authentic cultural exchanges.

Our History

Founded in 2023 in Nyeri, Kenya, Authentic Expeditions began as a passionate endeavor by local safari experts who wanted to share their deep knowledge and love for Kenya's breathtaking landscapes and vibrant cultures.

What started as a small team of guides has grown into a respected travel company, but we've never lost our original commitment to authentic, personalized safari experiences that benefit both travelers and local communities.

Today, we're proud to welcome guests from around the world while maintaining the personal touch and local expertise that set us apart.
